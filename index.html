How does online therapy work?

+

Sessions are conducted via Google Meet or Zoom in a secure, private video call. You need a smartphone or laptop, a stable internet connection, and a quiet private space. The meeting link is included in your booking confirmation email.

Is online therapy as effective as in-person?

+

Research consistently shows that online therapy is equally effective as in-person therapy for most conditions — including anxiety, depression, stress, and relationship issues. Many clients find attending from home actually makes it easier to open up and engage consistently.

How long does therapy take?

+

This varies by your goals and situation. Some clients see meaningful progress in 6–10 sessions for a specific issue; others benefit from longer-term support. We'll discuss this openly during your first session and revisit it regularly as we work together.

Is everything I share kept confidential?

+

Absolutely. Confidentiality is the foundation of therapy. Everything shared in our sessions is strictly private. The only exceptions are situations involving imminent risk of harm to you or others — a standard legal and ethical requirement for all mental health professionals.

What if I need to cancel or reschedule?

+

You can reschedule or cancel up to 24 hours before your session at no charge via the link in your Calendly confirmation email. Cancellations within 24 hours may attract a 50% fee. Life happens — just communicate early and we'll work it out.

What languages do you offer sessions in?

+

I'm comfortable conducting sessions in English, Hindi, Telugu, and Kannada. Many clients prefer a natural mix of languages — that's completely fine. The goal is for you to feel at ease expressing yourself however feels most natural.
